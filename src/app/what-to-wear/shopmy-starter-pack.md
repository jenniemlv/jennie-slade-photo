# ShopMy Starter Pack — 8 Outfit Collections

Each section is one ShopMy Collection. Once built, paste the resulting
`https://shopmy.us/collections/{id}` URL into the matching outfit's
`shopMyUrl` in `data.ts`.

Editing rule: every category carries one budget pick (`$` under $50), one
mid-range (`$$` $50–$200), and at most one splurge (`$$$` over $200) —
every link a direct product page, every choice defensible on taste:
matte, draping, weight, no synthetic shine, no logos. Brands span
Target/H&M/Quince on the accessible end through Vince/Toteme/Sezane on
the elevated end. ShopMy network confirmed for every brand listed here
as of last public partner sweep.

**Tier key:** `$` under $50 · `$$` $50–$200 · `$$$` over $200.
