JENNIE SLADE A Note on Investment
01A Note on Investment

What you are
actually buying.

A photograph is a small thing, until it is the only thing left of a season you cannot get back.

When you book a newborn session, you are not buying an hour. You are buying eighteen years of practiced eyes, a steady presence in your home on a very tender week, and a set of images you will hold onto long after the swaddle is outgrown.

My promise to you is simple. You will look like yourselves. Your baby will look like a baby, not a prop. The light will be soft and the morning will move at your pace.

Below are three ways to invest in this season. Every collection includes my signature warm, hand-edited style and a beautifully delivered online gallery. There is no right or wrong choice. Only what feels like the right fit for your family.

I am honored you are even thinking about this.

Jennie

JENNIE SLADE How to Reserve
05How to Reserve

Saving your
date.

Newborn dates are flexible by nature. Reach out anytime in your second or third trimester and we will hold a tentative window.

i.
Reach out.

Email or use the contact form on jennieslade.com. Share your due date and the collection you are drawn to.

ii.
Reserve.

A signed contract and $200 retainer hold your tentative window. The retainer applies to your collection total.

iii.
Welcome baby.

Once your little one arrives, message me and we lock in the actual session date around feeding and sleep.

iv.
Final payment.

Balance is due the day of the session. Major cards, ACH, and Venmo all welcome.

A Note on Pricing

Pricing is current for 2026 and may change with the calendar year. Once your retainer is received, the rates in this guide are locked for your session.
