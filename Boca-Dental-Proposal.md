## Project Overview

I'm excited to put together a plan for all 12 Boca Dental and Boca Kids Dental locations across Las Vegas and Northern Nevada.

Each location gets the full treatment: exterior shots, every interior space, staff headshots, and real action moments. Everything is delivered in individual folders organized by location address, so nothing gets mixed between offices.

I've had the chance to shoot several Boca Dental locations already, and I'd love to build on that across the whole network.

---

## What You Get at Each Location

**Exterior**
Multiple views from various angles to showcase the building and signage.

**Interior**
Every room, multiple angles. Waiting room, welcome desk, patient rooms, equipment, window views.

**Staff Headshots**
Doctors, hygienists, front desk team. I recommend natural window light for a consistent, polished look across all locations.

**Action Shots**
The real moments. Staff greeting patients, phone calls, cleanings, exams, ortho consultations. Whatever feels natural and is permitted.
