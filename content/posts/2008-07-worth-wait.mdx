our baby boy has arrived safe and sound. here's how he got here:<div> </div><div>
</div><div>i went in to be induced around 6:00 am and they hooked up the smallest dose of pitocin at 7:00am. I decided to go along with the rest of the birth plan and try for a natural birth. Things were going fine, my doula and Chad were there and we decided to play a game of <a href="http://www.trollandtoad.com/pd1640321.html?associateid=31_7">Ticket to Ride.</a> Right after the game was over (I won!) I started feeling the contractions more and more and then they started to get down right crazy painful. I told them that I couldn't do it any more, that it was too hard, and I had to have something for the pain.  The nurse checked me to see how far I had progressed and I was already dilated to an 8.5  which meant it was too late for any kind of pain relief. I was able to focus a little better knowing that this is just what I had to do, and within the hour the doctor arrived, he broke my water and I was pushing.  It took about 4 pushes (a long with the most intense pain i have ever felt in my life,) and our little bundle of joy was here.</div><div>
</div><div>He weighed 8 pounds 7 ounces - 21 inches long and has a beautiful head of hair. we love him so much and are grateful he is so healthy.  life is good. </div><div> </div><div>As for now... his name is Levi Allyn Slade. That is what it says on the birth confirmation, but Chad still isn't sure he likes it, so for now I still call him baby...</div><div><span class="Apple-style-span">
