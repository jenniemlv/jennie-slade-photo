my jackson boy is 6 today... bittersweet if you ask me, I can't believe how fast he is growing up!
<img src="/journal/2008-05-jackson-boy/dd31407e7b.jpg" alt="" />
last night this is how our conversation went:

me and chad: "Jackson, what would you like for your birthday?"

jackson: " I really, really want a maker machine... you know, so I could say I want a banana and a banana would come out."

me and chad:  "That's really nice but, how about something that you may have seen at the store."

jackson:  "Oh, I know! I saw this really cool orange gun at the dollar store, but it costs a whole dollar."

me and chad: "anything else..."

jackson:  "lots and lots of balls, footballs, and soccer balls..... oh and a car so I could go anywhere I want to."

We love you Jax! Nobody has a mind quite like you, and you light up all of our lives!
