Kate's First Year Album is complete! I sent all of the digital pages to <a href="http://www.shutterfly.com/shop/product_c18000/Photo_Books">shutterfly</a> and had it made into an 8 x 8 book. I got it in the mail yesterday and it was so exciting to see it all finished. Her album is WAY more complete than the other kids, but more compact (It's 64 pages and has everything from her first year). I even scanned in little cards, certificates, ultrasound pictures and notes so that her album wasn't missing anything.  Ahh... it does feel nice to start something and finish it....
