I love to wear hats.  They make you look finished and hide the grease for the busy days. (Shoot... I just gave away my secret)  Anyways, I was searching for hats and went into <a href="http://www.hollisterco.com/hol/homepage.html">Hollister</a> thinking they would probably have some....
<div><span>Then I realized.... I'm Old. </span>
</div>It was loud, and it smelled like a high school prom filled with boys wearing way to much cologne.  When an employee asked if I needed any help finding anything, I said "What!?" three times because of the loud music and then said <span>"Yeah, you could help me by turning on the lights, opening the window, and turning down the music."</span>  She stared at me with that "This is the greatest store in the universe" glaze and walked away.  And, the hats didn't fit, they were too small....

<div><span>Well, I've completed the first step: ADMISSION
I admit it, I'm OLD.  </span>
</div>
