In case you didn't know... there is a crazy easy way to download and backup your instagram photos to your computer/hard drive.  Go to the website <a href="http://instaport.me/" target="_blank">Instaport</a> and the rest is pretty self explanatory.  First step is to choose what you want to back up, and the second step is to click download.  It downloads your photos to a zip file.  Once you unzip the file by clicking it, it creates a folder of all your photos.  This software worked perfectly for me and then I was able to create this collage in minutes in lightroom.  Now go download your precious photos.  </div>
