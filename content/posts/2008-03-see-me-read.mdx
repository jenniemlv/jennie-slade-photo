I was lucky enough to be asked to review a sample kit for <a href="http://www.seemeread.com/index.cfm">"See Me Read."</a>  I got it in the mail last week and I must say, it was really cool.  You can customize all of the products with your own child's faces, friends, and things.  (I know my children love to be the star...) My kids especially liked the flashcards because I just tossed them in my purse and they could play with them... even the 2-year-old.  So basically<a href="http://www.seemeread.com/index.cfm"> "See Me Read" </a> gets 5 stars and you can check out their website <a href="http://www.seemeread.com/index.cfm">here.</a>
