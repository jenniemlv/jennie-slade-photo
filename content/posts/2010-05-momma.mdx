Dear Mom,

Thank you for being the person I want to become, and for making me who I am today.  (all of the good parts anyway)  You are my friend and my example, and I couldn't have asked for a better mom.  Thank you for the homemade bread when I would get home from school, for sewing dress after dress for my girls, for watching my terror/ Levi, for letting use your pantry as a grocery store, for bringing me dinner when I'm overworked, and for being my momma.  Love you mom.

Jennie
