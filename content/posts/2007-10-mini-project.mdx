I have an addiction. I am addicted to my <span>white can of spray paint.</span>  I can't resist when it calls me and says, "Spray more white Jennie, spray, spray spray...." The latest victim was a plastic black and gold oval frame that was on its way to the trash. It held a silhouette of me when I was 5 years old at Knotts Berry Farm and has been in a box for the past 15 years. I decided to try to salvage it before buying a new frame for the silhouette.  (I wish I took a picture of the before, but remember it was black, gold and plastic) I sprayed it white, added a turquoise accent with acrylic paint, and then put two coats of high gloss varnish. Here it is on Tay's wall.
