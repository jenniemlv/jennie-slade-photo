levi is now 9 months and i think to call him "crazy" is an understatement. he is ready to take on the world at full speed. unfortunately, i can't always accommodate him as quickly as i would like to and occasionally he gets neglected. saturday was an ultra-busy day and as i was running around getting taylor ready for her dance competition, levi got put in the only place that will keep him in one spot (other than his crib).  when i came back downstairs... this is what i found. face splatted on the high chair tray, drool streaming, and cheerios stuck in his hair and hands... and what do i do instead of immediately getting him out, bathing him, and rocking him to sleep again?   i grab the camera. .... poor baby.
