My sweet Kate was baptized in April.  Such a beautiful day and such a sweet daughter I have.  I couldn't be more lucky.  Here are a few shots from our last minute effort to take some photos.  We did it in 
about 10 minutes in the backyard with a backdrop, and Kate was a dream 
model as usual. My mother made her dress... yes, I know you're jealous. 
 Kate designed the whole thing, and we found the lace and trim at <a href="http://www.heddysfabrics.com/" target="_blank">Heddy's Fabric.  </a>  Her beautiful necklace was customized by the amazing artist James Dixon of <a href="http://goldesign.com/" target="_blank">Goldesign. </a>   We all love this little angel and couldn't be more proud.

To purchase baptism cards, you can head to my <a href="https://www.etsy.com/shop/patricklane">Etsy Shop.</a>

And if you are in the Las Vegas area or want to see what my travel schedule is for Baptism photos, check out my <a href="https://jennieslade.com/">Photography Website. </a>
