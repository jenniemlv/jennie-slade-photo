On a whim, I decided to take the kiddos to Lehi, Utah to visit my amazing aunt Lisa. Today, we had some fun visiting quilt shops, furniture stores and of course the <span><a href="http://thanksgivingpoint.com/museum/index.html">Dinosaur Museum.</a></span>  Kate kept calling the Dinos, "cute bunnies".... strange.... we made fossils and saw a 3D dinosaur movie. What a fun place!

Poor Jackson was so worried about Dad being home all alone.  He said, "What will he eat?, He doesn't know how to make food?" I told him Dad would be just fine and would probably go out to eat.  He was still worried and said, "Who will tell him to go and get something to eat?"

We miss you Daddy, hope you aren't starving......
