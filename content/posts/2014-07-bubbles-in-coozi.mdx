<div class="separator">
Some of my favorite memories of all time were spent at my Grami's house.  Grami knew how to make us feel loved and welcomed and I always felt like she was so glad that I came.  </div>
<div class="separator">

</div>
<div class="separator">
We had a ritual in the summer time:  We would go to Grami's, swim in her pool, come inside and eat lunch and watch "Days of Our Lives"  (Grami's favorite "program") Then we would head back out swim in the pool, then go in the Jacuzzi.  The best was when Grami let us have bubbles and they spilled out everywhere.  </div>
<div class="separator">

</div>
<div class="separator">
Bubbles and hot snickerdoodles, it doesn't get much better. </div>
<div class="separator">

</div>
<div class="separator">
Now my Mom is passing on the tradition of bubbles in the coozi and my kids are always overjoyed when the spa turns into a big white fluffy marshmallow. </div>
<div class="separator">
