<div>i love that i have 2 boys.  even being 6 years apart, i can see their "guy connection."  levi and jackson share a room and it works surprisingly well.  jackson can fall asleep even when levi is still up talking and gabbing about baseball, and when levi wakes up in the morning as jackson's alarm clock, jackson throws in some balls, and legos as his way of pushing snooze.  Eventually the two make it downstairs and Jackson turns on PBS kids (disobediently), makes them both a bowl of cheerios and they sit down to eat together.  It's pretty darn adorable...
