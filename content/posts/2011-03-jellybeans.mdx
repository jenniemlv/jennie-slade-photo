kate said to me the other day:

"Mom, I think I am allergic to jellybeans."

me:  "Why would you think that Kate, did you eat some?"

kate:  "No, but every time I look at a picture of jellybeans, or see them on the computer, or even think about them I cough, or sneeze."

me:  "Yep, you are definitely allergic."</span>
