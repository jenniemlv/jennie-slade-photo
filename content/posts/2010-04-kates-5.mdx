My baby girl is 5 today. We are all grateful her birthday <span>finally </span>came.  Kate has been asking since Thanksgiving how many more days until her birthday, and I think we had all had it! She could hardly sleep the night before because of the excitement.

She woke up to royal treatment and crepes (her breakfast request) on china while the rest of us ate on paper.  She wore a royal crown which I purchased from the talented Jennifer of the etsy store <a href="http://www.etsy.com/shop/Joosycardco">Joosy Card Co.</a> (It was a coincindence that she is from Las Vegas and not too far from me.  She may even join the Girl Next Door Show next time...Check out her store it's pretty awesome. )

Kate was beside herself with pure giddiness and sat at the breakfast table for 30 minutes until it was ready.  She had an exciting day full of friends and family and lots of presents. Happy Birthday Katie Pie!
