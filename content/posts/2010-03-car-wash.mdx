<div><div>this weekend, one of the chores was to "clean out the car."  let's just say, the kids interpreted it how they wanted to.  before i knew it, there were buckets, soap, and swimsuits.  i laughed as chad tried to teach them the <span>proper</span> way to wash a car.  all they wanted to do was play with the bubbles and water, and by the end we were left with a car full of soap streaks and chad re-washing while shaking his head.
