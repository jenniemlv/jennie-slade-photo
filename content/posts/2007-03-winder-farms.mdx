I am always on the hunt for good, healthy, non-processed, fresh foods.  I have recently been getting my milk and eggs from <a href="http://www.winderfarms.com/">Winder Farms </a>out of Utah. It is so nice to wake up to fresh milk, (with no added hormones or antibiotics) and brown cage-free eggs on my porch. Their prices are really reasonable and the added health benefits for my family are really worth it. Unfortunately they only deliver to Utah and Las Vegas, but I am sure there are fresh farm delivery services through out the country. If you are in Vegas or Utah, definitely give it a try!
