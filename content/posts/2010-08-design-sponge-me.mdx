The majorly famous design blog <a href="http://www.designspongeonline.com/2010/07/before-after-lauren-nicks-chair-jennies-chandelier.html">"Design Sponge"</a> featured <a href="http://focusjennie.blogspot.com/2010/07/chandelier-makeover.html">my chandelier</a> under their before and afters.... I'm blushing.   I have had many questions asking for the source of the light bulbs:  They are regular light bulbs found at Home Depot, Lowes, Target, Walmart etc.  They are the large, round/globe light bulbs and the ones I purchased are from GE.  I don't have the box to remember the exact name but they aren't hard to find, just look for the biggest.  Usually they are used for bathroom track lighting.
