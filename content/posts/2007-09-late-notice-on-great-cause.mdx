This is pretty late notice, but in case any of you are interested and live in Vegas, this sounds like a great event, for a great cause...

On September 21st - 23rd, more than 35 countries, hundreds of yoga studios and thousands of yoga practitioners worldwide will gather as a part of the Global Mala Project. <span><a href="http://one.org/">ONE</a> </span>Nevada will co-host a Yoga-thon at IN2YOGA in Las Vegas in conjunction with the Global Mala Project. The project aims to raise awareness in the worldwide community for some of the most essential issues of our times, such as HIV/AIDS and the AIDS crisis in Africa.

<span>WHO: Members of the Las Vegas community joining a global family in making a difference by donating time, money and spirit to groups alleviating poverty.
WHAT: Global Mala Yogathon to alleviate poverty. For more information, visit www.globalmala.org
WHEN: Friday, September 21 through Sunday, September 23, with classes beginning at various times throughout the day and early evening. For complete local schedule see below or call 702-312-1101 or 702-309-9642.
WHERE: IN2YOGA Studio
9330 W. Sahara Ave. Suite 250
Village Square Shopping Center, off of Fort Apache and Sahara
Las Vegas, Nevada
