Well, as you probably all know by now, <a href="http://www.marthastewartcrafts.com/index.php">Martha Stewart</a> has launched her much anticipated craft line.  I knew it would be good, but it is <span>REALLY </span>good.  I went to Michael's over the weekend and<span> </span>was in awe at all the great products, and fabulous packaging. I am actually a little torn because now the chase is over. The days of searching for cute food bags that I could use for gifts, and buying 100 from a wholesale food packaging site so I could have 10, are over. The days spending too much time making labels for gifts are over.... etc. etc. Martha has made it easy for all of us and the chase is over....
