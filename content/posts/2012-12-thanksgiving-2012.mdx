Thanksgiving 2012 was pretty dang fantastic!  Miraculously, every single member of the Slade Family made it.  Chad worked hard to secure a location in BrianHead where all 33 of us could fit, an it was a great vacation.  We had a couple oven issues, but other than that, we enjoyed a fantastic Thanksgiving meal, snowboarding/skiing, sledding, lots of games, and lots of fun.  It could be years before everyone is together again.  Definitely a time to be remembered! </div>
