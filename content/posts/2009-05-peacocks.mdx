me, kate, and levi headed off to the Floyd Lamb State Park on Friday because she had an incessant urge to feed ducks . not only did she feed the ducks and the geese, but realized that she LOVES peacocks. she didn't even seem to mind that they didn't love her back and continued trying to hug, chase and pet them. who knew with a mom like me... i would have such an animal loving child.  just makes me grateful that i haven't rubbed off on her too much.
