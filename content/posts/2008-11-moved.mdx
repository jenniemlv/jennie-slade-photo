<span>we have moved out of the old house, and into the new. </span>
<span>the house is still a construction zone, and i think i'm getting the flu.</span>
<span>i am sitting on a box working on chad's computer.</span>
<span>in my family room is a pink barbie scooter.</span>
<span>my email isn't working; the gas didn't get hooked up,</span>
<span>i'm missing my MAC and i've had enough.</span>
<span>halloween was great, pictures to follow,</span>
<span>i need a hot shower... maybe tomorrow?</span>
<span>it will be worth it, i know it will;</span>
<span>i'm just hoping i get through it without needing a pill.</span>
<span>adios to you all, sweet dreams and good night,</span>
<span>i'm off to my dusty bed to close my eyes tight.</span>
<span>as grouchy as i sound i'm grateful for a home,</span>
<span>for a wonderful family and for this silly poem.</span>
<span>for my kiddos, my husband, good chocolate and guacamole,</span>
<span>i'm finishing this now before it gets too boring...</span>
<span></span>
<span>(i must be losing it... i just wrote a poem about my move?)</span>
