My kids are so great, they gave me such wonderful cards and hugs for Mother's Day, and Taylor made me a really cool flower vase and tissue flower (I wanna make one myself now!) They even made Chad make me some eggs... what sweet kids!  I have been asking for a bike for a while and Chad picked out a pink beach cruiser all by himself, I know he wished I wanted a Lance Armstrong professional bike like the rest of his <a href="http://www.redburroracing.com/">possee...</a> , but I think he cycles enough for the both of us.... I can't wait to go out riding bikes with the kiddos, me with Kate in the stroller just couldn't keep up with Tay and Jax.
