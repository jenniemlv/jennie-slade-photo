i wanted to invite you all to the grand opening of <a href="http://www.patricklane.etsy.com/">"Patrick Lane,"</a> my new etsy shop! Patrick Lane is the street I grew up on.  It is the street where I made fun memories, lasting friendships, and where I started my first business ventures: ( lemonade stands, Rock stores, dance classes in the garage) Patrick Lane will always hold a special place in my heart, hence the name of my shop. I hope to update the <a href="http://www.patricklane.etsy.com">shop</a> regularly so that I can keep my creative juices flowing. If you see something you like and you live close, just email me and I can bring it by, (so you don't have to pay the shipping.) Thanks for taking a look. Enjoy your stroll down <a href="http://www.patricklane.etsy.com/">Patrick Lane...</a><div> </div>
