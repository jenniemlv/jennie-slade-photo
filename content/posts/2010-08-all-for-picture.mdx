I have come to realize, that I am so busy taking every ones pictures that my own children don't seem to have any "professional" pictures of themselves.

There are many reasons for this:
- They really don't like the camera any more (it's sort of their enemy)
- I am tired of trying
- My silly antics don't work on them
- I am always around them so their cuteness wears off and I forget they need their pictures taken.

I tried desperately to take Levi's 2-year old pictures the other day with him sitting in front of a white backdrop.... it didn't happen, not even close. This is what we ended up getting instead:
