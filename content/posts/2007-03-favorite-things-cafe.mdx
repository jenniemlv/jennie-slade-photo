For a recent church activity, our theme was "My favorite things."  We decided to have dinner pre-packed in cute white boxes (from <a href="http://smartandfinal.com/">Smart & Final</a>) tied with a bow for a picnic-like feel.  I designed a sticker for the front of the box; "Favorite Things Cafe" and some magnets for everyone to take home.  I also really wanted turquoise doilies, but no one sells them (surprise!) so I painted them with turquoise acrylic paint. Everyone shared one of their favorite things ($5 limit) It was a fun night for us girls with all the girly colors around tasty homemade desserts.  (---- The designs on the magnets and stickers are from Katie Pertiet at <a href="http://designerdigitals.com/">Designer Digitals</a> and Michelle Coleman at <a href="http://scrapartist.com/">Scrap Artist</a>.)
