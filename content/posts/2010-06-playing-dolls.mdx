My aunt Georgia was thinking that no one would want her old doll furniture... crazy! We jumped at the chance and we couldn't be more grateful that she let us have it.  Kate has had countless hours of fun, and all the kids in the neighborhood ask to play with Kate's "special doll stuff." I love the way Kate decorates and sets up her house... it's pretty adorable.  It's amazing when you think of all the work that went into these beautiful pieces so many years ago.  They will be passed on for generations.  Thank you Georgia!
