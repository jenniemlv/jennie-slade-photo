Jackson got a cool "human dissecting kit" for Christmas, but he thought it was grotesque. Kate on the other hand was very intrigued.  She <span>is</span> always the first one to ask to see a bloody nose, a scrape on the knee, a bump on the head or stitches, but I had no idea how much she would love this <span>thing. </span>
She has spent hours daily working and "doing her surgery" as she calls it.

She told me: "When I am a doctor in a hospital, I am going to have to take this with me so I remember where everything goes.  I want to put everything back the right way."

smart girl.

She  came home the other day from a long day of school, dance and friends and said, " I am so tired, can I just do some surgery now?"
