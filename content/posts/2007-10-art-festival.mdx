Over the weekend, I took the kiddos to the <a href="http://www.celebrationoftheartslv.com/">Summerlin Art Festival</a>, while Chad worked on making room to fit both cars in the garage. (After our "welcome to the neighborhood car break-in" last week, he thought it would be a good idea.)
Taylor found a painting that she loved and was so excited that she was able to meet the artist, <a href="http://www.debbiemariegallery.com/">Debbie Arambula</a>.  She now wants to "collect her art because she knows her." 
I love the cultured adult in my little girl.
