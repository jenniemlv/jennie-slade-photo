<a href="http://www.inke.nl/index2.php?lang=en">Inke Heiland</a> from the Netherlands has designed a very playful and creative way to decorate your walls.  Using vintage wallpaper from the 60's and 70's they have created a number of different wallpaper designs that are pre-cut and ready to assemble.  These designs will make any room unique and beautiful.  I can't wait to get in our new home, I want to put three trees in my little girl's room.  You can find these products at <a href="http://www.rompbklyn.com/html/items/wallpaper_tree.html">ROMP</a>.
