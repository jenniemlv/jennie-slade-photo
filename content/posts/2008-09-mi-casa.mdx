<img src="/journal/2008-09-mi-casa/927bf2c574.jpg" alt="" />hi, my name is levi. welcome to a little place i like to call home. i know you are probably feeling sorry for me right now, but don't. i actually enjoy my cozy little abode. think about it... in these tight quarters i actually get to do quite a lot; i go to dance classes, soccer games, to and from preschool and elementary school. i go to the grocery store, costco, the park, singing lessons, the mall, and target. now how many of you out there can say that you can go to all of these places from the comfort of your home?... that's what i thought.  i also can eat and poop all from this same spot. if you ask me, i'm pretty lucky to have such a nice "mobile" home and to get to see the world through the back seat of a tahoe. i bet your jealous now.
