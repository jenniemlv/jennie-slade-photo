</div>My sister-in-law made this salad and I was rather blown away with its tastiness.  So good.  So simple.

<span><span>Asian Noodle Salad</span></span>

Ingredients:
- Cooked spaghetti or angel hair pasta
- peppers of every color
- green onions
- unsalted roasted peanuts
-whatever else you feel like putting in there
- {You can add cooked chicken if you are looking for more of a full meal}
- Trader Joes "Asian Style Spicy Peanut Vinaigrette"
(don't try to substitute another dressing... only this will work)

Mix it all together.  That's it.  Yum.
