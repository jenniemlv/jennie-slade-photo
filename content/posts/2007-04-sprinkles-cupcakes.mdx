This cupcake bakery, <a href="http://www.sprinklescupcakes.com/index.html">Sprinkles</a>, looks like such a fun place. The building is simple, minimalist, and adorable. It makes me want to run down to Beverly Hills, Phoenix, or one of there other locations just to get a cute cupcake. I was quite excited to see that they will be coming to Las Vegas; we need a touch of <span>innocent</span> fun around here!
