I have had this magnet board for 3 years now, and it has always had a nice home in the corner of the garage.  I finally fixed it up and hung it up in Tay's room where it was meant to be 3 years ago.

<span>1.  Start with unfinished wood framed magnet board (I think I bought this at <a href="http://www.robertscrafts.com/index.php">Roberts Craft</a>)</span>
