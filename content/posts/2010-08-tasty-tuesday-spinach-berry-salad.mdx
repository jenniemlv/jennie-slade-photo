<span><span>Spinach Berry Salad</span></span>
<h2><span>Ingredients</span></h2>   <ul class="ingredients"><p><span> 4 packed cups torn fresh spinach
1 cup sliced fresh strawberries
1 cup fresh, or frozen, blueberries
1 small sweet onion, sliced
1/4 cup chopped pecans, toasted
Salad Dressing
2 tablespoons white wine vinegar, or cider vinegar
2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
2 tablespoons honey
2 teaspoons Dijon mustard
1 teaspoon curry powder (can be omitted)
1/8 teaspoon pepper  </span></p></ul>   <h2><span>Directions</span></h2><span> In a large salad bowl, toss together spinach, strawberries, blueberries,  onion and pecans. In a jar with a tightfitting lid, combine dressing  ingredients. Shake well. Pour over salad and toss to coat. Serve  immediately.     </span>
