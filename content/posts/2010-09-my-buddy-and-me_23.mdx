it's just me and levi at home now... all the kids have gone to school til 3:30 and it's just Levi and me.   I love my little buddy.  We hang out at the park, have long lunches, do lots of errands and grocery shopping, fold laundry and play cars.  Until 3:30 comes when Taylor walks in the door saying, "Levi, I'm home, " and he then runs with his arms wide open giving her a huge squeeze, (and then repeating the process with Jackson and Kate) he's all mine.  I am going to savor my time with my little man, even if he is counting down the second until 3:30 finally arrives.

my buddy and me.
