I am so excited! I finally figured out how to hard boil and egg!  I have done it many times before, but the eggs always having a dark gray/green tint around the yolk and the whole thing is dry.  But now.... you can call me the "Eggspert"  (egg expert). I owe it all to my dear friend <a href="http://www.marthastewart.com/">Martha</a>, who has taught me this important necessity for Easter.  And might I say, the eggs taste SO much better.  <a href="http://www.marthastewart.com/portal/site/mslo/menuitem.fc77a0dbc44dd1611e3bf410b5900aa0/?vgnextoid=a0fc428e3ea0f010VgnVCM1000003d370a0aRCRD&autonomy_kw=hard%20boil%20egg&rsc=ns2006_m1">Try it out</a> for yourself and enjoy your eggs on Easter.  And for those of you who think you already know how to make hard-boiled eggs.... hard-boiled eggs are never actually boiled..... So if you didn't know that tid bit of info, you better <a href="http://www.marthastewart.com/portal/site/mslo/menuitem.fc77a0dbc44dd1611e3bf410b5900aa0/?vgnextoid=a0fc428e3ea0f010VgnVCM1000003d370a0aRCRD&autonomy_kw=hard%20boil%20egg&rsc=ns2006_m1">re-learn</a> how to do it. Bon Apetit!
