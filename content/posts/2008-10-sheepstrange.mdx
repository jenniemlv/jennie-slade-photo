yesterday me and my "world famous" tennis team, were off to Boulder City (an hour away in the middle of no where) for our tennis matches. we pulled up to the courts and to our surprise, there was at least 50 big horn sheep surrounding the courts. us city girls were a little, well a lot in shock and thought the whole scene was rather crazy.  when our competition showed up, they acted like it was no big deal and said "just don't make sudden movements, or coax them and they won't RAM you".... huh? maybe they didn't know i am not the biggest animal lover, and i definitely wasn't loving the idea of horns ramming into me.... needless to say, we all survived and had some good laughs about how the "big horn sheep" team kicked our butts and how we still have yet to win a match...
