A birthday banner is super duper easy to make, so I kind of feel silly even posting a tutorial on it, but here it is.

1.  Pick a font.  I love to use <a href="http://www.dafont.com/romantiques.font">Romantiques</a> by dafont.com for banners. (go download it.... it's free)
2.  On photoshop, illustrator  or a word processing program fit 2 letters on a page.  (make sure the document you are working on is set up in landscape mode/ horiontally)
3.  Pick the color letters you want.
4.  Print on card stock.
5.  Cut in half with a <a href="http://www.amazon.com/Fiskars-9698-12-Inch-Portable-Trimmer/dp/B00019DTRQ">paper cutter</a>.
6. Punch two holes on the top of each letter.
7.  String ribbon through.
8.  Add a little glitter.
9.  Hang.
10.  You now have a birthday banner.
