Today's recipe is all about "kids in the kitchen."  Taylor, my 10 year old made these 100% by herself.  She got a little cook-book in her stocking that I am guessing Santa got in the $1 section as a stocking stuffer, and she really likes trying things in it.

No, these are not homemade sugar cookies, which are obviously the better choice... but like I said, she is 10 and did them all alone. And let me just tell you... these were dang good, store bought cookie-dough and all.

So let go of your pride and give your kids a shot at making <a href="http://www.pillsbury.com/Recipes/ShowRecipe.aspx?rid=18864">mini-fruit pizzas</a> for a fresh treat.
