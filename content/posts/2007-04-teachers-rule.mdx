Taylor's first grade teacher had her last day today ( she is going on maternity leave).  We are going to miss her so much! She truly loved each one of her students as she inspired them, taught them, encouraged them, and gave them a confidence and love for learning.  What more could you ask for in a teacher! I made a blanket for her new baby that matched the room decor. It turned out very girly and sweet just like her Momma.
