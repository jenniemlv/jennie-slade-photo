I don't really know why we celebrate St. Patrick's day, but any reason  to celebrate is a good one in this house.  The kids woke up to a bit of  craziness left by a mischievous leprechaun.  Their were chairs turned  upside down, dolls sitting on motorcycles, headphones on a robot, a high  chair on a table and plenty other things of such nonsense. Jackson did  his best to make a trap, he really didn't want his room messed up, but  after all of his effort that little leprechaun managed to leave a note  and escape... "2 quik 4 u. me fast."  Jackson has already got plans for  next years trap.  We had a traditional irish breakfast and enjoyed each  other and the sunlight as we put on our green duds and got ready for a  beautiful 75 degree sunny day.  I also made 70 of<a href="http://focusjennie.blogspot.com/search?q=rainbow"> these</a> and  delivered them to the kid's classes (really, what was I thinking when I  started this tradition) Hope the luck'o the irish was with you this St.  Patrick's Day!
