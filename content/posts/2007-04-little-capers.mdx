As much as I love Disney and other cartoon and T.V. characters, I cannot let my children wear them on their clothes. I know I should let them have self-expression, but this is one area where I don't budge. I have caved into letting them have character underwear, or character pajamas (cause no one really sees them), but that's it! <a href="http://www.littlecapers.com/home.html">Little Capers</a> has designed a great product where the kiddos can still be heroes, but not walking advertisements. Some of my favorites are the <a href="http://www.littlecapers.com/shirt_pk_earth.html">Earth Heroes</a> and the <a href="http://www.littlecapers.com/shirt_red_star.html">Red Star Heroes</a>.   Too cute!
