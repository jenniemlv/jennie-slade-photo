i know this may not sound like big news to you utahns or coloradons or new yorkns, but today... in the desert.... it snowed,.... it snowed big time.

<img src="/journal/2008-12-snow/bb258b8b87.jpg" alt="" />
we were all so giddy and it took every ounce of my strength to not go get the kids out of school. i was heartsick sending them off to school knowing that would be stuck in a classroom with no windows, and couldn't watch the beautiful snowfall. chad reassured me that the snow would still be here when school was over. ( mr. husband, thank you for being the sensible half)

<img src="/journal/2008-12-snow/695599505c.jpg" alt="" />
<img src="/journal/2008-12-snow/51b3a78580.jpg" alt="" /><img src="/journal/2008-12-snow/e276e0d5c2.jpg" alt="" />

<img src="/journal/2008-12-snow/6f07003299.jpg" alt="" /><img src="/journal/2008-12-snow/5d7737c081.jpg" alt="" />chad ran to the store and got some sleds so we were ready. they got home, we put on our random hand-me-down selection of snow clothes and we were off to the park across the street to play our hearts out in the snow.

we sledded, ate snow, rolled around....
