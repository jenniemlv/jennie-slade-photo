after 2 months of beautiful 75 degree - sunny weather, today it is cloudy and rainy. as much as i have enjoyed the sun, rainy days make me happy. (probably because they are rare here in the desert.) everyone slept in a little longer because the sun didn't wake them up, and levi slept in until 9:30. I was getting a little anxious for him to get up and kiss those warm cheeks and smell his "just woke up after 13 hours of sleep" smell. finally i heard him talking in his crib, grabbed him out and we cuddled up some more on my bed with all the windows open so we could hear and smell the rain. kate joined in on the action and says, "aren't rainy days 'bluetiful."  yes they are. today we will be making cookies, pies, thanksgiving crafts and of course finding some time for "chillaxin" on this "bluetiful" rainy day.
