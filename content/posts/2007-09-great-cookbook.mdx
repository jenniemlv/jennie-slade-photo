<a href="http://www.amazon.com/World-Wisdom-Amy-Cox-Jones/dp/B000UTFVNC/ref=sr_1_2/002-4667322-7435256?ie=UTF8&s=books&qid=1190767193&sr=1-2"><span>A WORLD OF WISDOM,</span> </a>by Amy Cox Jones is wonderful cookbook, for all of you health-conscious mommys.  It definitely isn't your average cookbook as recipes are separated by season. Living in Las Vegas, where pretty much nothing is "home-grown" I find myself not even knowing when things are in season (which of course, is when we should be eating them). This book straightens it all out focusing on a grain based, low animal product, whole food diet. The recipes are super tasty, the author is a sweetheart, and I love having it as a reference as I try to feed my family what's best.  <span>
