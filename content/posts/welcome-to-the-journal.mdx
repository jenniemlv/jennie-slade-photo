I am so glad you found your way here. This little corner of the site is where I get to slow down and share the stories behind the sessions. Not just the pretty photos, but the real stuff. The laughing toddler who wouldn't sit still. The senior who was nervous at first and then absolutely shined. The family that cried happy tears looking at their gallery.

## Why a Journal?

After twenty years of doing this, I have so many stories. And for a long time, they just lived in my heart. A journal felt like the right way to share them. Not a blog with SEO tips or a business update. A journal. A place where I can write like I talk, share what I love, and hopefully give you a little window into what it's like to work with me.

I also want this to be useful. If you're thinking about booking a session, I want you to feel like you already know me a little before we even meet. And if you're a current client, I hope you see yourself in these pages.

## What to Expect Here

You can look forward to session sneak peeks and full galleries once clients give me the green light to share. I'll write about what to wear for different seasons and locations around Las Vegas. There will be behind-the-scenes posts about the spots I love to shoot at. And honestly, some posts will just be me sharing something that made me happy that week.

### What's Coming

- Family session stories from our favorite Las Vegas outdoor locations
- Senior portrait features (with permission, of course)
- Seasonal style guides for what to wear to your session
- Location spotlights around the Las Vegas valley

I'm starting small and letting it grow naturally. That feels right.

If you've been thinking about booking a session and something you read here spoke to you, I'd love to hear from you. [Let's connect](/contact) and start planning something beautiful.
