taylor, jackson, and me spent the last two friday nights at the lovely, "Miss Kim's" house learning to paint a "funky family portrait." taylor chose to do a painting of her and kate, jackson chose a self-portrait and i decided to do one of the whole family. mine is only about a quarter of the way done. (that's after 7 hours of painting, so you can see how slow i am).  we had a fabulous time and i loved seeing jackson and tay's creative minds at work. can't wait to go to another class. thanks kim!
