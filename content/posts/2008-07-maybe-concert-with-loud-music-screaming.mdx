We did have an awesome time at the American Idol concert. My favorite, Jason, sang "Over the Rainbow," and i loved every second, and I thought Michael Johns and David Archuletta did great also. For all those people who think American Idol is too "trendy" or "not cool enough," you are missing out. My family and I had a great time enjoying music together and cheering our favorite singers on.  Kate loved seeing Brooke White on her piano and was adorable as she clapped along to the music. .... We are all excited for the next season to begin, but for now "So You Think You Can Dance," keeps us all excited for Wednesday nights.
