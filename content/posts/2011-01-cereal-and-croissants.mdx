<span >INA GARTEN's CROISSANT BREAD PUDDING</span>
<h2>Ingredients</h2>   <!--concordance-begin-->   <span class="nocoupons">nocoupons</span><ul><li class="ingredient">3 extra-large whole eggs </li><li class="ingredient">8 extra-large egg yolks </li><li class="ingredient">5 cups half-and-half </li><li class="ingredient">1 1/2 cups sugar </li><li class="ingredient">1 1/2 teaspoons pure vanilla extract </li><li class="ingredient">6 croissants, preferably stale </li><li class="ingredient">1 cup raisins</li></ul>   <!--concordance-end-->   <h2>Directions</h2>  <div class="instructions">   <p> Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F. </p>   <p> </p>   <p>In a medium bowl, <a onclick="'s_objectID=" href="http://www.foodterms.com/encyclopedia/whisk/index.html" class="crosslink" debug="64 68">whisk</a> together the whole eggs, egg  yolks, half-and-half, sugar, and vanilla. Set the custard mixture aside.  Slice the croissants in half horizontally. In a 10 by 15 by 2 1/2-inch  oval <a onclick="'s_objectID=" href="http://www.foodterms.com/encyclopedia/cookware-and-bakeware-materials/index.html" class="crosslink" debug="248 258">baking dish</a>, distribute the  bottoms of the sliced croissants, then add the raisins, then the tops of  the croissants (brown side up), being sure the raisins are between the  layers of croissants or they will burn while baking. Pour the custard  over the croissants and allow to soak for 10 minutes, pressing down  gently. </p>   <p> </p>   <p>Place the pan in a larger one filled with 1-inch of hot water.  Cover the larger pan with aluminum foil, tenting the foil so it doesn't  touch the <a onclick="'s_objectID=" href="http://www.foodterms.com/encyclopedia/pudding/index.html" class="crosslink" debug="722 728">pudding</a>. Cut a few holes in the  foil to allow steam to escape. Bake for 45 minutes. Uncover and bake for  40 to 45 more minutes or until the pudding puffs up and the <a onclick="'s_objectID=" href="http://www.foodterms.com/encyclopedia/custard/index.html" class="crosslink" debug="887 893">custard</a> is set. Remove from the  oven and cool slightly. Serve warm or at room temperature. </p>  </div>
